Concentration Optionality for Harvard Student Athletes


We believe that course times in certain concentrations limit concentration choices for student athletes at Harvard.


We wanted to look deeper in to this issue. So...


  1. We looked at data from the Harvard Open Data Project on Concentrations,
  2. used a web scraper to obtain similar data for athlete concentrations,
  3. and used D3 to visualize the possible differences between the two.
